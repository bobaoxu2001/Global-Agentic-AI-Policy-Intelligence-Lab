**Would this project alone justify an offer? No portfolio can establish that.** An offer decision still needs evidence of the candidate’s actual language ability, stakeholder relationships, cross-functional performance, submitted work, judgment under organisational constraints, and real outcomes.

## Evidence still required from the candidate

These are not defects to “polish” inside the repository. Adding simulated proof would reduce credibility.

1. A real example of influencing a product, policy, market, or leadership decision.
2. A legitimately submitted consultation, association paper, event contribution, or stakeholder engagement.
3. Evidence of sustained monitoring and priority-setting over multiple weeks or months.
4. Actual cross-functional collaboration, conflict resolution, and outcome ownership.
5. Personally supportable language, regional, and relationship experience.
