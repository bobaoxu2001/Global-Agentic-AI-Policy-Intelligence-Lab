Methodology

How the numbers mean what they mean

The ADRS formula

Inherent = Σ (weightᵢ · scoreᵢ · 25) over A(.25) T(.20) D(.20) E(.15) R(.20), each scored 0–4 against fixed anchors.

Residual = Inherent × (1 − MitigationCredit), where MitigationCredit = min(0.40, Σ credits). Residual can never fall below 60% of inherent — the anti-gaming property (MD-9).

Final = min(100, Residual × J), where J = 1 + Σ components, capped at 1.30. J only ever raises the score.

Half-open tiers, tier-from-raw

Low [0,25) · Moderate [25,50) · High [50,75) · Critical [75,100]. Full precision internally; display 1 dp; tier derived from the unrounded score — a badge may read "50.0 · High" (MD-8).

Jurisdiction is separate from J

Selecting a jurisdiction reveals reference material only. The four J components — binding_hit, near_term_hit, enforcement_posture, prohibition_adjacent — are set by the analyst, not auto-applied. enforcement_posture and prohibition_adjacent are always ▲ inference (MD-3).

Epistemic discipline

Every claim is exactly one kind: ■Fact (cited, confidence), ▲Inference (based_on chain, confidence ≤ min of sources), ●Recommendation (rests on a chain, no confidence chip, never "makes you compliant"). Mixed-kind blocks are forbidden. Fact confidence and mapping confidence are recorded separately (MD-7).

Jurisdiction-invariance

A, T, R are identical across a scenario's four assessments (they describe the system); only D and E may diverge, and only with a written justification (MD-12).

Accessibility notes

Semantics never carried by color alone — glyphs (■▲●), shape (bindingness), and text labels back every palette; verified for protan/deuteran/tritan.

Body text ≥ 4.5:1, large text/UI ≥ 3:1 on paper surfaces. Live ADRS panel is an aria-live="polite" region. Segmented controls are real buttons with aria-pressed; tables expose aria-sort.

Full keyboard path; visible focus rings; evidence panel focus-trapped when drawered, dismiss on Esc; prefers-reduced-motion disables skeleton shimmer.
