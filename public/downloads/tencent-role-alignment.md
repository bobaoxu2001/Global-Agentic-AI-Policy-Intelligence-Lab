## What the project demonstrates

- A disciplined distinction between policy fact, analytical inference, and recommendation.
- The ability to translate agent capabilities into questions for policy, product, security, and engineering teams.
- Executive synthesis that includes opportunity, not only compliance risk.
- Source and lifecycle hygiene: bindingness, dates, confidence, last verification, and public correction paths.
- Good judgment about limits: no fabricated stakeholder activity, no company affiliation, and no claim that a draft corpus authorizes launch.

## Remaining gaps

- Independent human legal/policy review is still needed before the research corpus can be called publication-ready.
- The structured corpus is narrower than the original MVP target and has thin provision depth in some markets.
- Real stakeholder engagement and internal cross-functional collaboration must come from actual experience, not a portfolio simulation.
